Welcome to my journey

My name is Abiodun Adenike, i've always had a passion for storytelling which led to pursue a degree in Mass Communication at Lagos State University. However, after graduating, i felt a pull forward the dynamic world of technology. Encouraged by my analytical skills and eagerness to learn, i enrolled at AltSchool Africa to begin my journey into tech.
The transition wasn't easy. I found myself navigating unfamiliar terrain, learning to code, and grappling with concepts that often seemed daunting. Yet, i embraced each challenge with determination, seeing every obstacle as an opportunity to grow. The supportive community at AltSchool Africa helped me realize that i wasn't alone in this journey.
During late-night study sessions, R&B music became my constant companion, soothing my mind and keeping my spirits high. When i needed a break from screens, i immersed myself in novels, letting stories whisk me away to different worlds.
